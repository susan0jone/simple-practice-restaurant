ABOUT US

Nibble is a cozy restaurant in Western Nigeria offering

take-out and patio dining for the fall of 2020.

Located in one of Lagos’ most historic buildings.

Nibble opened in 2009 as a family style restaurant

and serves Mediterranean BBQ-inspired brunch and dinner selections,

homemade sweets and fresh fruit cocktails along with wine pairings and craft beer.

The folks at Culinaria invite you to pop over,

relax and celebrate one of life’s greatest joys
